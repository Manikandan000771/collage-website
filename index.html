SYED AMMAL ARTS & SCIENCE COLLAGE

Syed Ammal Arts and Science College is run by the Syed Ammal Educational Trust established by the father of Ramanathapuram Dr.E.M.Abdullah in the name of his beloved mother Syed Ammal. He was a famous doctor, a well-known philanthropist and a social worker.


DEPARTMENTS

  • COMPUTER SCIENCE
  • MATHAMETICS
  • PHYSICS
  • ENGLISH


All admissions into the College are made as per the guidelines of Alagappa University and Government of Tamil Nadu. Application for admission into the College should be submitted only in the prescribed form available in the College office. Normally, admissions are done only once in a year in the beginning of the academic year.
